## âš¡ Workflow: From Script to Screen

### Step 1: Prep
Open the Sidebar.
1.  **Add Cast**: Upload images for the characters in this scene.
2.  **Set Style**: Upload a style reference for the "Final" render.

### Step 2: Direct
1.  Paste your scene script into the **Script Editor**.
2.  Click **"Chunk Script"**.
    *   *The app will automatically break the script into a list of individual shots.*

### Step 3: Generate
Scroll through the Shot List. For each shot:
1.  **Select Cast**: Check off which characters are in this specific frame.
2.  **Sketch**: Click **Draft** to see a rough idea.
3.  **Render**: Click **Final** to turn that sketch into authorized production art.

### Step 4: Iterate (Free Render)
Need to fix a specific detail?
1.  Go to the **"ğŸ¨ Free Render / Revise"** tab.
2.  Upload your own rough drawing (or a corrected draft).
3.  Describe the scene and click **Render**. The AI will "paint over" your sketch using our locked visual style.
